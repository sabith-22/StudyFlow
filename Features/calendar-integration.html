StudyFlow's Calendar Integration feature brings your personalized study plans to life in an intuitive calendar format. This powerful visualization tool helps you see your study commitments alongside other life events, ensuring a balanced approach to time management.

With easy navigation between daily, weekly, and monthly views, you can plan ahead, identify available study blocks, and maintain a holistic view of your academic and personal schedules.

Planning Made Easy

The Calendar Integration makes planning your study routine simple:

  • Drag and drop study blocks to reschedule sessions when conflicts arise
  • Set recurring study sessions for consistent subjects or topics
  • Block out non-study time to ensure your study plan works around your other commitments
  • View completed and upcoming study sessions to track your adherence to your plan
  • Export your calendar to share with study groups or academic advisors

With StudyFlow's Calendar Integration, you'll never miss a study session and can maintain the perfect balance between academic success and personal well-being.
